Lū'au Bay

Where the Whales Sing and Hearts Expand

"E hoʻokahi ka puana" - Many voices, one song

Welcome to Paradise

A fictional island sanctuary between Maui and Molokai, where love expands rather than divides and the whales bring magic home.

🌊 The Setting

Founded in 1887 as a whaling station, transformed into a sanctuary town. Features dramatic black lava cliffs meeting pristine white sand in a perfect crescent bay, backed by emerald mountains.

🐋 Whale Season Magic

Every year, the humpback whales return, bringing with them a season of transformation, romance, and possibility. The town believes anything can happen when the whales sing.

💕 Love Without Limits

Lū'au Bay embraces polyamory and all forms of ethical love. Here, hearts expand rather than divide, and the community supports every authentic expression of connection.

Meet the Locals

Auntie Mahina

The Wise Elder

70-year-old kahuna (spiritual leader) who's been in a loving triad for 40 years. Runs the community garden, officiates all kinds of ceremonies, and advocates for "love in all its forms."

The Beach Boys

Kai & Pono

Two 60-something best friends who sit outside Moonfish Mercantile playing ukulele. Former lovers who remained best friends and serve as unofficial town historians.

Coral

The Town Connector

30-something social media influencer who moved here from LA. Runs the town's Instagram, organizes pop-up events, and is a vocal advocate for ethical non-monogamy.

Luna the Cat

The Matchmaker

A massive Maine Coon who appears to live everywhere and nowhere. Shows up at significant romantic moments. Local legend says she's the reincarnation of an ancient matchmaker.

Dr. Marcus Chen

The Skeptic with a Heart

Marine biologist studying whale communication. Came for research, stayed for love (though he won't admit it). Provides scientific grounding but slowly opens to the town's magic.

Magical Places

🌅 Sunrise Sips

Open-air café where morning yoga classes spontaneously happen on the deck. Famous for their "heart-opening" cacao ceremonies and acai bowls.

💎 Siren's Pool

A natural infinity pool where sea meets fresh water. Site of the monthly Full Moon Sound Baths.

🏨 The Driftwood Inn

Only "hotel" in town - 8 rooms each named after a sea goddess. Famous for its clothing-optional hot spring pools and weekly "cuddle puddle" movie nights on the lawn.

🌙 Three Lovers' Cave

A sea cave where legend says three ancient lovers discovered they could share one heart.

🌺 The Floating Gardens

Tidal pools transformed into a botanical wonder.

🌳 The Blessing Tree

Ancient banyan where people tie wishes on ribbons.
